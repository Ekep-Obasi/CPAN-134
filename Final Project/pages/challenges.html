Meatless Monday Challenge
Food
Beginner
4 weeks
2,834 participants
14.2 tons CO₂ saved

Description

The Meatless Monday challenge encourages you to avoid meat products one day a week. Livestock production is responsible for about 14.5% of global greenhouse gas emissions, so reducing meat consumption is one of the most effective ways individuals can lower their carbon footprint.

By participating in this 4-week challenge, you'll not only reduce your environmental impact but also explore delicious plant-based alternatives and potentially improve your health.

How It Works

  1. Every Monday, avoid all meat products (beef, pork, chicken, fish, etc.)
  2. Log your meatless meals in the tracker
  3. Share your favorite plant-based recipes with the community
  4. Track your carbon savings over the 4-week period
  5. Complete all 4 Mondays to finish the challenge
